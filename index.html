About Us

We are family-owned restaurant serving fresh Mexican-inspired dishes and classic American comfort food in a cozy environment. We offer breakfast and lunch as well as the pleasure of enjoying the best food in Indianapolis! We have a wheelchair accessible entrance as well as a wheelchair accessible parking lot, restroom, and seating. We can do curbside pickups, takeouts, dine-ins, and deliveries. We have fast service, great coffee, and we serve local specialties! Our restaurant is popular for breakfast, brunch, and lunch, as well as solo dining, comfort food, and a quiet and cozy atmosphere. We offer counter service, table service, and even a bar onsite! For Payments we take credit and debit cards. Our parking is also free so come enjoy at any time!
